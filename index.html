Fun Solar System Facts

  • Jupiter is so big all planets could fit inside it.
  • Venus rotates in the opposite direction of Earth.
  • Mars has the tallest volcano: Olympus Mons.
  • Saturn can float on water due to low density.
  • Neptune has the fastest winds in the solar system.
  • The Sun contains 99.8% of all solar system mass.
